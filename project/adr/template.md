# ADR-XXX: <Short, Descriptive Title>

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated  
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

Describe the problem or decision context.
Focus on why this decision matters and why it is difficult or costly to change later.

Avoid describing implementation details.

## Decision

State the decision clearly and unambiguously.

Use normative language:

- “We adopt…”
- “We prohibit…”
- “We require…”

## Constraints

List the rules and invariants introduced by this decision.
These constraints must be enforceable and testable at a conceptual level.

Examples:

- “All X must…”
- “X is forbidden unless…”
- “Introducing Y requires a new ADR…”

## Reversibility clause

Explain:

- under which conditions this decision should be revisited,
- what would trigger a new ADR,
- and whether reversal is expected to be exceptional or routine.

## Consequences

Describe the expected outcomes and tradeoffs.
Include both positive effects and known costs or risks.

Keep this high-level and factual.
